### Feature Definitions
- `average_monthly_income`: Calculated as the mean of all credited transactions categorized as income over the last 90 days, annualized.
- `current_account_balance`: Real-time primary checking account balance.
- `credit_utilization_ratio`: (Total outstanding credit card balance / Total credit limit) for all active credit lines.
- `num_failed_transactions_30d`: Count of all debit transactions (e.g., direct debits, card payments) that failed due to insufficient funds within the last 30 days.
- `time_since_last_login_days`: Number of days since the customer's last successful login to any digital banking channel (web, mobile).
- `direct_deposit_flag`: Boolean indicating presence of recurring direct deposits in the last 60 days.
